About Ibeto

We support and assist the communities where we do business, hold ourselves to the highest standards of ethical conduct and environmental responsibility.

Our Vision/Mission

  • To become a global company admired for its superior quality products and services.
  • To engage in the manufacturing and distribution of high quality products that best meet the needs of local and international markets with a fair return to our stake holders.
  • To provide rewarding careers for our Employees through a quality work environment that assures their commitments to the Organization and enables them to actualize themselves.
  • To remain an exemplary and corporate citizen.

Our Philosophy

  • Whatever is profitable must also be honest, and whatever is honest must also be profitable. To separate profit from honesty is to pervert the first principle of nature.

Our Approach

  • We take care of our people and our people take care of the business.

Our Values

  • Customers : We listen to our customers and improve our products to meet their present and future needs.
  • People: Our success depends upon high-performing people working together in a safe and healthy workplace where diversity, development and team work are valued and recognized. We take care of our people and our people take care of the business.
  • Accountability: We expect excellent performance and are accountable for our actions and results. Our leaders set clear goals and expectations. Always supportive, they seek and provide frequent feedbacks.
  • Citizenship: We support and assist the communities where we do business, hold ourselves to the highest standards of ethical conduct and environmental responsibility.
  • Financial Responsibility: We are prudent and effective in the use of the resources entrusted to us.

Strength & Strategies

  • Clear vision and challenging goals.
  • Attention focused on our business rather than our competitors.
  • Short response time to challenges.
  • Prudent in management of resources.
  • Excellent Team Spirit/Hardwork.
  • Making quality assurance a key step in every aspect of our operations.
  • Relentless pursuit of full customer satisfaction.
  • Empowering our workforce by giving them a sense of self worth.
  • Striving to deliver fair returns to our stakeholders.
